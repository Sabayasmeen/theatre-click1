PRIVACY POLICY

This policy describes what personal information we collect and how we use it. This Privacy Policy applies to all information received by Theatre for a audience, both online and offline, written, or verbal communications.

Our Commitment :

We will not your share names or personal information with any other entity, nor send mailings on behalf of other organizations. This Privacy Policy applies to all information received by Theatre for a audience, both online and offline, including any written or verbal communications.

The Information We Collect :

Theatre for a audience collects information when you visit our website, purchase tickets. Some of this information is technical information automatically logged by our servers. Outlined below are the types of information, both directly from you and indirectly through the collection of information by third-party service providers that we specifically collect.

Personal Information :

For purposes of this Privacy Policy, Personal Information means any information that identifies or could be used by or on behalf of Theatre for a audience or our third-party service providers to identify a natural or legal person. Personal Information does not include information that is encoded, aggregated, anonymized, or publicly available information that has not been combined with non-public personal information. In addition to the Personal Information, we may collect additional information that does not personally identify you.
