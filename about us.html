WANT TO KNOW MORE ABOUT
THEATRE CLICK ?

The Theatre Click, originally an Italian Embassy ballroom, was opened by Princess Ilyana in May 1949 as a theatre for the staging of live performances. For the past 4 decades it has been operating as a cinema on the alternative circuit appealing mainly to the more discerning viewer who enjoys its quality product and the charm of its old-world ambience. In addition to the conversion to digital projection, The Ilyana has been able to continue slowly upgrading her facilities to make your experience an even more memorable one.

Keep following our work in progress as the bar, terrace and catering facilities continue with their upgrade and offer you exciting and more diverse eating and drinking options to choose from.
